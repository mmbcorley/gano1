Information About the Experiment

This project has been approved by PPLS Ethics committee at the University of Edinburgh. If you have questions or comments regarding your rights as a participant, the committee can be contacted at ppls.ethics@ed.ac.uk.

Nature of the study. You are invited to participate in a study which involves identifying spoken words. There will also be some questions about your experience with English. Your session may last for up to 20 minutes.

Risks and benefits. There are no known risks or tangible benefits to you from participating in this study.

Confidentiality and use of data. We do not ask for your name or other personal information. The anonymous data collected during this study will be used for research purposes only, in accordance with Data Protection law. A public version of the data will eventually be made available at osf.io/rvp48/.

Right to withdraw. You may withdraw from this study at any time and for any reason. If you withdraw from the study after your data has been recorded, we will delete your data as long as you can provide the anonymous ID we will assign you within two weeks of completing the study. You will not be penalised in any way for withdrawing.

If you have any questions about what you've just read, please contact Martin.Corley@ed.ac.uk for more information.
